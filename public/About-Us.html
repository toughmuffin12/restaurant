History


We have crafted our fish sandwich recipe for years, culminating in the finest fish sandwich the world has ever seen. We start of by giving the live fish a bubble bath. Next, we filet the fish and slow roast it for 42 hours, 13 minutes, and 7 seconds for optimal crunchiness. Then we add our secret ingredient and wala, our fish is ready to be consumed by are beautiful costumers.
